Pondok Pendawa – Center for Religious, Scientific and Entrepreneurial Training

Profile

Pondok Pendawa hadir sebagai pusat penempaan keagamaan, keilmuan dan keahlian bagi pemuda dan alumni pondok pesantren agar mampu mandiri dan berdaya guna.

“Sebaik-baik manusia adalah yang paling bermanfaat bagi manusia lainnya.”

Kurikulum memadukan kajian keislaman klasik, penguatan karakter, serta keterampilan kewirausahaan agar santri siap menghadapi tantangan zaman.

Vision, Mission and Competencies

Vision

To be a center for religious, scientific and entrepreneurial training for youth and pesantren alumni.

Mission

  • Organizing religious and entrepreneurship education.
  • Strengthening academic skills and practical experience.
  • Becoming a development center for youth from remote and border areas.

Santri Competencies

  • Strong in Islamic knowledge.
  • Professional in vocational skills.
  • Creative, independent and solution oriented.
